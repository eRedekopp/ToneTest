This directory contains the results of the ToneTest piloted July
15-16, 2019. Single puretone. Calibration: 6 vols per freq, 10 trials
per vol, frequencies {200, 500, 1000, 2000, 4000}Hz. Confidence: 1 vol
per freq, 20 trials per vol, frequencies {220, 880, 1760, 3520}

Subject 50 was tested on July 24 with an updated version of the study
that tests louder volumes (tagged 1.0.1 on Git) - results not
represented in compiled spreadsheet
